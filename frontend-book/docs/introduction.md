# Introduction to Physical AI Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the Physical AI Humanoid Robotics Textbook! This comprehensive guide will take you through the fundamental concepts and practical implementations of humanoid robotics.

## About This Book

This book is designed for AI students and developers entering the field of humanoid robotics. Each module builds upon the previous, providing both theoretical knowledge and practical implementation skills.

## Modules Overview

- **Module 1: The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)** - Learn about ROS 2, the communication backbone of robotic systems
- Additional modules covering kinematics, control systems, perception, and AI integration

## Getting Started

Begin with Module 1 to establish a solid foundation in ROS 2 concepts, which form the backbone of most humanoid robotics systems.
